description::TwitForce integrates with Twitter to change game dynamics. TwitForce is a SHMUP with a twist. Tweets which contain #TFPilot and tweets that contain #TFEnemy will provide boosts to the pilot fire rate and enemy spawn rates respectively. This is a multiplayer SHMUP of a totally different sort and Connects the Worlds of Twitter and Gaming!
