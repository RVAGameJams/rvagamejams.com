description::You and two of your species have arrived on an island, explore the valleys, coastlines, mountains and ruins in search of other potential tribesmen in order to cultivate a following. Choose between bloodshed and passiveness to see the effects herd mentality has as a result of following by example. Controls, WASD for directional movement Choose between “join us” and “kill” by clicking on the prompts
