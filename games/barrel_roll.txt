description::Your ship evolves as you play the game. Pick up weapons and have parts of your ship break off and re-structure itself as you navigate a narrow corridor. 

Use A/D to steer your ship down the alley. 

Hit Enter to fire your weapon. 

Thanks for checking it out. 


To play: 

download the .jar and double-click to run. 

if that doesn't work, try running it from the command line with: 

java -jar barrel-roll.jar
